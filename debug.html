Only a Gemini Will Do

K.L. Hall

4.56
271 ratings 24 reviews
Rate this book
Three months ago, Kareem Dorsey was supposed to be nothing more than a distant memory.

Just the handsome stranger who put me through the mattress in the middle of a hurricane.

The career criminal who made me laugh almost as hard as he made me c*m.

NOT the father of my unborn baby.

But fate doesn’t care about the safeguards we put around our hearts or the lines we drew in the sand.

It only cares about the here and now.

And right now? He’s everything I’m not supposed to want in a man—impulsive, too charming for his own good, dangerous, and not to mention a Gemini.

And when he finds out about the baby he put inside me? All bets are off. At least for him.

Will he be able to convince me to take a chance on love for the sake of our unborn child, or will the mistakes of his past keep us apart forever?

Was this a little messy? YES! And i loved it!

When you love someone, you just do. It's that unexplainable feeling. Sometimes, you might even do crazy things for that person you love. The suspense throughout the book for me was whether Sawyer and Kareem would get their happily ever after. I couldn't figure out which direction the author was headed with their story. What I will say is, I swear the twin, Kadeem, yielded magical powers.

Sawyer's village (her sister, 2 best friends) who loved and embraced her was available. Her sister Soleil was that big sister who didn't play about her little sister. She was hilarious. She was serious. She was gentle, yet funny. Displayed no judgment, although she could have.

I liked how this story was written and, in my opinion, was created into parts.
Part 1. in Brazil and the reveal
Part 2. her return home from Brazil & being with best friends
Part 3. Kareem's return & his rearrest; Christmas w/sister
Part 4. the sister & mother love (baby shower & return for birth)
Part 5. birth of baby Kareena
Part 6. ?????????? whatever you want or think

Loved that the writer focused more on the positive traits of Kareem vs. the negative. He really did want to be there for Sawyer and baby Kareena and become the man and daddy they would be proud of. Now, how was this gonna happen after being taken into custody again?

You'll have to read to find out the conclusion of Sawyer's and Kareem's story.
